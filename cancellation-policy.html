E-Bike Rentals

Rental Cancellations & Reschedules

24+ hrs → full refund Text 330-406-9686

Cancel or reschedule with at least 24 hours' notice for a full refund. Inside 24 hours we may not be able to refund the full amount, but we'll always work with you on weather, illness, or genuine emergencies. Just text the rentals desk as soon as you know — 330-406-9686.

The sooner you tell us, the more we can do — a same-day cancellation is much easier to work with than a no-show.

Rain or Shine

Weather Cancellations

If we cancel for weather, you get a full refund or a free reschedule — your call.

Light rain isn't usually a reason to cancel; lightning, severe storms, ice, or unsafe trail conditions are. We'll reach out the morning of your ride if it looks dicey, so keep an eye on your phone.

Safety First

Rider Readiness

Every rental starts with a full tutorial. If we don't feel confident in your readiness during that tutorial, we'll cancel the rental and offer a full refund. Guest safety is non-negotiable — we reserve the right to refuse a rental to anyone we feel is unfit to ride an e-bike. These are 50-pound machines that reach 20–28 mph, and we'd rather send you home safe than send you out unsure.

Holds & Damage

Deposits & Damage

Multi-day and long-term rentals carry a $200 refundable hold placed on the card at pickup. It's released on return, assuming the bike comes back in roughly the same shape it left.

Normal wear is on us. Rider negligence — collision, drop damage, or missing parts — is on you, per the waiver you signed at booking. We'll assess any damage when you return the bike and let you know if there's a charge before billing anything. We don't surprise people with fees.

Service & Maintenance

Tune-Ups & Service

Once work has begun, parts and labor are non-refundable. If we haven't started yet, you can cancel any time at no charge.

If you're not happy with the work, talk to us — we stand behind every tune-up and will make it right.

New Bike Purchases

Returns on a New Bike

Reach out before you buy if you're unsure — that's exactly why test rides exist. Once a bike has been ridden outside the shop, we treat it as used, and any return is handled case-by-case. We'd much rather you take a longer test ride than end up with a bike you don't love.

A warranty issue is separate from a return. If something's wrong with your bike, bring it in (or text photos and a description) and we'll handle the claim with the manufacturer on your behalf — being an authorized dealer means we cut out the back-and-forth.

Try Before You Buy

Test Rides

Test rides are free — no deposit, no commitment, nothing to cancel. If your plans change, just let us know so we can free up the bike for someone else.
