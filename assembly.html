02. The "Ohio-Proof" Protection

Specialized corrosion protection against Northeast Ohio's harsh conditions.

Northeast Ohio is tough on bikes—salt from winter roads, spring humidity, summer grit. Our Rust-Belt Shield treatment applies specialized corrosion protection to your frame, bolts, and vulnerable components.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Creek Ready Package required?
No, but it's highly recommended. You can choose to assemble your bike yourself, but you'll miss out on the safety certification, rust protection, break-in tune-up, and the peace of mind that comes with professional assembly. Most customers choose Creek Ready because the value far exceeds the cost.
What if I already bought my bike online?

No problem! We can still apply the Creek Ready Package to Heybike, Velotric, or Jasion bikes purchased elsewhere.

How long does the setup take?
Proper Creek Ready assembly takes 3-4 hours of hands-on work. We don't rush—quality takes time. Most bikes are ready for pickup within 3-5 business days from when we receive them.
What's included in the 30-day tune-up?
Within 30 days of pickup, bring your bike back for a complimentary checkup. We'll re-torque all bolts, adjust brakes and gears, check tire pressure, and ensure everything has properly settled. This service alone is worth $75—and it's free with Creek Ready.
Does this void my manufacturer warranty?
Absolutely not. As an authorized dealer and service center for Heybike, Velotric, and Jasion, our assembly work is fully warranty-compliant. In fact, professional assembly often helps warranty claims because we document everything properly.
Can I watch the assembly process?
Due to workspace constraints and insurance requirements, we don't offer observation during assembly. However, we're happy to walk you through everything when you pick up your bike and answer any questions about the work performed.
What brands qualify for Creek Ready?
Creek Ready Package is available for all Heybike, Velotric, and Jasion models. For other brands, contact us—we may be able to accommodate depending on the model and our familiarity with the system.
