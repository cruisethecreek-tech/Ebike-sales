Bridge the Gap - Our Mission

What Does It Mean to "Bridge the Gap?"

Transportation is about more than just getting to work—it's about accessing the essentials of a good life.

While most of our riders use their e-bikes to get to a job, we know that transportation is about more than just a paycheck. In the Mahoning Valley, "The Gap" is the distance between you and the essentials of a good life. Here are the other ways we bridge it:

🏥

The Healthcare Gap

Missing a doctor's appointment because your ride fell through shouldn't happen. An e-bike provides independent access to local clinics and pharmacies. No more waiting on a bus schedule or an expensive Uber when you need to pick up a prescription.

🛒

The Food Security Gap

Youngstown has many "food deserts" where a grocery store is miles away. Walking for 45 minutes with heavy bags is impossible for most. Our bikes, equipped with rear racks and panniers, let you carry a week's worth of fresh groceries home in minutes, helping you bridge the gap to healthy, affordable food.

🎓

The Opportunity Gap

Whether it's evening classes at YSU or trade school training, your future shouldn't depend on when the WRTA stops running. An e-bike gives you 24/7 access to education, allowing you to bridge the gap toward a better career on your own time.

📉

The Financial Gap (Ditching the Car)

Even if you have a car, the cost of gas, insurance, and maintenance can keep you in a cycle of debt. Replacing a second car with an e-bike can save a family hundreds of dollars every month.

The Math:
Our $30/week program is a fixed cost that eventually leads to full ownership—no surprise $500 repair bills.

Ready to Bridge Your Gap?

Join the Creek Crew and gain the independence you deserve.
No credit checks. No license needed. Just reliable transportation.
