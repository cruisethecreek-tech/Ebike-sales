Kirk Road Trailhead

Canfield, Ohio

This award-winning trailhead provides a fifty-car parking lot, restrooms, water fountains, a picnic pavilion, and a location for educational and trailside activities.

The 11-mile Mill Creek Bikeway is owned and operated by Mill Creek MetroParks — paved and tranquil end to end.
