How to Take a Card Payment in Person

Every invoice you create works two ways — the customer can pay the online link, or you can run their card right here at the shop with the Stripe Reader. It's the same invoice either way; you just pick how to collect.

Take a card in 5 steps

  1. Create the invoice
    Use the invoice tool as usual. It creates a real Stripe invoice for the customer.
  2. Open the Stripe Dashboard app
    On your phone or tablet → tap Invoices → find the invoice you just made.
  3. Tap “Collect payment”
    Then choose your paired Stripe Reader from the list.
  4. Customer taps, inserts, or swipes
    The reader handles the card. Wait for the green checkmark.
  5. Done — it's automatic
    The invoice flips to Paid, the sale is logged, and the customer's receipt emails itself.
Remember You do not need the payment link to take a card in person. The link is only for customers paying online later. In person = Dashboard → Collect payment → Reader.

First time each day

  • Power on the reader (hold the button until lights come on).
  • Open the Stripe Dashboard app → Terminal.
  • Confirm the reader shows as connected.
  • If not, tap Connect a reader and pick it from Bluetooth.

If something goes wrong

Reader won't connect
Make sure Bluetooth is on and the reader is charged. Power it off and back on.
Card declined
Ask for another card, or send the customer the online payment link instead.
Receipt didn't arrive
Check the email on the invoice was correct. You can resend from the Dashboard.
